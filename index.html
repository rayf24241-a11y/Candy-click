About

A classic idle clicker game inspired by the genre. Click a rusty old candy bucket to collect candy. Spend candy on buildings that auto-generate more candy, and buy upgrades to boost your clicking power. Features 6 buildings and 5 upgrades, with an auto-save system so you never lose progress.
